	\begin{abstract}

		\paragraph{Highlights}

		\begin{itemize}

		\item We conducted a field experiment among 311 low-income individuals comparins a prize-linked savings account (PLS) and a standard interest-bearing account.
		\item Individuals saving with PLS made 42\% more deposits on average compared to those with standard savings accounts. 
		\item By varying feedback on lottery results, we find some evidence that the treatment effect is consistent with regret aversion.
		\item Recent experiences of regret induce more subsequent deposits.
		\item We document evidence that use of PLS results in a 15\% increase in gambling activity.

		\end{itemize}

 	\end{abstract}
